About GALKHAFIZ

Artist Galina Khafizova (aka GALKHAFIZ) from Tel Aviv has been active since the 2014 on a scene of fine art, internartional fashion magazines, starting practices that are now taken for granted, such as using house walls as a canvas for her work. A highly versatile artist, Galina works with painting, Illustrations, and photography. Her thesaurus paintings show overlapping synonyms executed in old master techniques, togehter with mixed substances, while other pieces often take a single world, repeated for effect.
